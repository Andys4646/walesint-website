01 The Firm

Two decades of turning aspiration into acceptance.

Wales International is a British Council–trained and certified education consultancy, working since 2003 to place ambitious students at the world's leading universities. Our counsellors don't hand you a brochure — they build your case, choose your path, and stay with you until the visa is stamped.

  • Efficiency. Streamlined, technology-led processes that move fast and waste nothing.
  • Transparency. Honest advice and clear communication at every step — no surprises.
  • Solutions. A path built around your goals, not a one-size template.
